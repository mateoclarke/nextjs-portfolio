# Portfolio

These are a few projects and products I'm proud to have helped with.

You can find my work history on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mateoclarke/)

- [Universal Preschool](https://upk.colorado.gov/)
  - I served as the Engineering Lead during the implementation of the technology to support the Colorado Department of Early Childhood launch a free preschool program for all 4 year olds in the state.
- [Vision Zero ATX](https://visionzero.austin.gov/viewer/map)
  - I led the software team that developed internal and public data tools targeting the end to fatalities and serious injuries related to mobility in Austin .
- [Census 2020](https://github.com/cityofaustin/census2020)
  - I made a website and reporting tools to support marketing and outreach for the 2020 Census as a joint initiative between the City of Austin and Travis County.
- [Budget Party](https://github.com/open-austin/budgetparty#readme)
  - I led a team of designers and developers to create an open-source participatory budgeting app and game night event.
- [Academic Works Scholarship Platform](https://www.blackbaud.com/solutions/grant-and-award-management/award-management)
  - I served as a product engineer on a platform that helped colleges and foundations allocate scholarship funds to students.
- [Casar for Council](https://github.com/mateoclarke/casarforcouncil) & [Keep Austin Affordable](https://github.com/mateoclarke/keep-austin-affordable)
  - I built campaign websites for candidates and bond initiatives.
